The aim of this website is to provide a room-booking service for both individuals and institutions alike. Companies can provide their facilities and people can view those listings and book one that suits their needs.

On the client side:

  • You can select which institution's rooms you would like to book
  • You are able to view available rooms
  • You can book a room
  • You can view rooms you have already booked

On the corporation side:

  • You can register rooms you would like to let people book
  • You can view booking requests and you can deny/accept them
  • You can edit room information
  • You can remove room bookings
